Steps to Obtain Social Security Retirement Benefits
Apply online at ssa.gov/retirement

Create a My Social Security account

Complete the application with personal, work, and banking info

Submit supporting documents (birth certificate, W-2s, etc.)

Receive confirmation and benefit estimate

Wait for approval and first payment notification

Steps to Enroll in Medicare
Visit medicare.gov or call 1-800-MEDICARE

Apply online through your Social Security account

Choose the plan: Part A (hospital), Part B (doctor visits), or both

Decide on additional coverage (Part C/Medicare Advantage or Part D)

Submit your application

Receive your Medicare card in the mail
